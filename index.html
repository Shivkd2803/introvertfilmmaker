Kael Morrow · Cinematographer
The Story

A Life
Behind
the Lens

I grew up in Cape Town, where the light has a quality unlike anywhere else — golden, heavy, impossibly cinematic. By seventeen, I had borrowed my father's Super 8 and was documenting everything.

After studying film in Prague and assisting on productions across four continents, I developed a visual language rooted in restraint — the belief that what you choose not to show is as powerful as what you do.
